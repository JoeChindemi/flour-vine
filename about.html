ABOUT

    Flour & Vine is a new-age Philadelphia pizzaria taking the city by storm. It was founded in 2022 by current owner Joe Chindemi, a Drexel Student who decided to follow a lifelong dream of his and open his own resturaunt. The concept of Flour & Vine was simple: make quality wine and quality pizza for the students and people of Philadelphia.

Flour & Vine has a classic menu that only includes two things: pizza and wine. Although our pies and wines are simple, they are some of the best around town. Our famous grandma slice has become a hit around University City, and everybody can't stop raving about the Merlot. We hope you can stop by and enjoy a bite or a sip!
